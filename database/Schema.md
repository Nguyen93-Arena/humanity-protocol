# Humanity Protocol Database Schema

## Overview

The Humanity Protocol Database is designed as a modular, scalable, AI-native data architecture.

Every component of the protocol stores only verified, meaningful, and privacy-respecting information.

---

# Core Domains

1. Users

Verified human accounts.

2. Identity

Proof of Humanity and identity verification.

3. Human Graph

Relationships between verified humans.

4. Trust

Trust score and trust evidence.

5. Reputation

Contribution history and reputation.

6. Opportunities

Jobs, projects, tasks, campaigns, learning.

7. Matching

Human-to-human and AI-assisted matching.

8. Value Routing

Intelligent routing of opportunities.

9. AI

AI agents and AI collaboration records.

10. Audit

Immutable activity logs.

11. Analytics

Aggregated ecosystem metrics.
