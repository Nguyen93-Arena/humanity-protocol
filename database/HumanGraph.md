# Humanity Protocol Human Graph Model

## Purpose

The Human Graph represents the trusted relationship network between verified humans, organizations, AI agents, and applications.

Unlike traditional social graphs, the Human Graph measures meaningful relationships based on verified interactions, collaboration, trust, and contribution.

---

# Core Concept

Every verified Human becomes a Node.

Every verified interaction becomes an Edge.

Together they create the Human Graph.
