Introduction

Every civilization is built upon relationships.

Families.

Communities.

Businesses.

Nations.

Yet today's digital world represents relationships in a limited way.

Social networks connect followers.

Humanity Protocol introduces a new concept:

The Human Graph.

Beyond the Social Graph

Traditional social networks measure popularity.

Followers.

Likes.

Shares.

Views.

These metrics describe attention.

They do not describe contribution.

The Human Graph is fundamentally different.

It represents meaningful human relationships built through verified interactions, trust, skills, knowledge, collaboration, and shared purpose.
