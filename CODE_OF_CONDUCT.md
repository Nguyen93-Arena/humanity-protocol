# Humanity Protocol Code of Conduct

> **Human First. Trust Always. Build Together.**

## Purpose

Humanity Protocol exists to build the Human Value Infrastructure for the AI Era.

Technology is not our final product.

People are.

Every contribution should strengthen trust, cooperation, and opportunity for real humans.

---

# Our Principles

## 1. Human First

Every decision should benefit real people before technology.

Technology serves humanity—not the other way around.

---

## 2. Truth Over Noise

We value facts, transparency, and honest discussion.

Disagreement is welcome.

Personal attacks are not.

---

## 3. Build Together

Every contributor is respected.

Ideas compete.

People do not.

---

## 4. Open Collaboration

Knowledge grows when shared responsibly.

We encourage learning, mentoring, documentation, and constructive feedback.

---

## 5. Respect Privacy

Humanity Protocol protects human dignity.

Personal information should never be exploited.

Privacy is a fundamental design principle.

---

## 6. No Harm

Do not use Humanity Protocol to:

- Spread misinformation
- Harass others
- Scam users
- Manipulate reputation
- Abuse AI
- Violate human rights

---

## 7. Long-Term Thinking

Build for decades.

Avoid shortcuts that damage trust.

Trust takes years to build and seconds to lose.

---

# Community Promise

We welcome contributors from every country, culture, language, and background.

Respect creates trust.

Trust creates opportunity.

Opportunity creates value.

---

# Our Belief

The world's most valuable infrastructure is not computers.

It is trusted humans connected together.
