# Humanity Protocol Specification

# HPS-004 — Opportunity Engine

Version: 1.0

Status: Draft

---

# Abstract

The Opportunity Engine defines how Humanity Protocol creates, discovers, verifies, and distributes opportunities across the ecosystem.

An opportunity is any verified request for human value.

---

# Definition

An Opportunity connects a verified demand with one or more qualified participants.

It is the starting point of value exchange.

---

# Objectives

The Opportunity Engine enables:

- Opportunity creation
- Opportunity discovery
- Intelligent matching
- Verification
- Fair participation
- Transparent outcomes

---

# Opportunity Types

Examples include:

- Employment
- Freelance Projects
- Business Partnerships
- Investment
- Mentorship
- Learning
- Research
- Community Service
- Volunteering
- Donations
- Competitions

The protocol allows new opportunity types through future extensions.

---

# Opportunity Structure

Every Opportunity contains:

- Opportunity ID
- Creator
- Organization (optional)
- Category
- Description
- Required Skills
- Location (optional)
- Time Constraints
- Reward
- Verification Status
- Visibility
- Creation Date
- Expiration Date

---

# Matching

Matching considers:

- Skills
- Reputation
- Trust
- Interests
- Availability
- Previous Contributions
- Geographic Preferences

Matching should maximize long-term value creation.

---

# Verification

Opportunity creators may be:

Verified Human

Verified Organization

Verified Community

Verified Application

Verification improves ecosystem trust.

---

# AI Integration

AI Agents may:

Discover opportunities

Recommend participants

Summarize requirements

Predict matching quality

AI never makes the final decision on behalf of participants.

---

# Privacy

Participants control which opportunities they receive.

Sensitive opportunity information must be protected.

---

# Summary

The Opportunity Engine transforms verified demand into meaningful collaboration.

It connects people, organizations, AI systems, and applications through trusted opportunities.
