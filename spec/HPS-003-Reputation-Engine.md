# Humanity Protocol Specification

# HPS-003 — Reputation Engine

Version: 1.0

Status: Draft

---

# Abstract

The Reputation Engine defines how Humanity Protocol measures long-term credibility based on verified contributions, expertise, and community recognition.

Reputation reflects what a participant has consistently accomplished over time.

---

# Definition

Reputation is the historical record of verified contributions and recognized achievements.

Unlike Trust, Reputation grows through sustained value creation.

---

# Objectives

The Reputation Engine enables:

- Long-term credibility
- Expertise recognition
- Better opportunity matching
- Community confidence
- AI-assisted recommendations

---

# Reputation Sources

Reputation may be influenced by:

- Completed projects
- Verified achievements
- Community recognition
- Professional experience
- Educational credentials
- Open-source contributions
- Mentorship
- Knowledge sharing
- Verified publications

---

# Reputation Profile

Each Human maintains a Reputation Profile containing:

- Contribution History
- Expertise Areas
- Achievement Records
- Community Recognition
- Organization Recognition
- Reputation Timeline

---

# Reputation Lifecycle

Reputation accumulates over time.

High-quality contributions strengthen reputation.

Verified misconduct may reduce reputation.

Historical achievements remain part of the record while reputation continues to evolve.

---

# Reputation Categories

Examples include:

- Technical
- Business
- Education
- Research
- Community
- Leadership
- Creativity
- Social Impact

Participants may have different reputation levels across categories.

---

# Relationship with Trust

Trust measures confidence.

Reputation measures historical achievement.

Trust supports future collaboration.

Reputation demonstrates proven experience.

Both systems complement each other.

---

# Privacy

Participants control the visibility of their Reputation Profile.

Applications receive only authorized reputation information.

---

# Design Principles

Evidence-based

Long-term

Transparent

Portable

Privacy-first

Human-owned

---

# Summary

Reputation is earned through continuous contribution.

It represents a participant's long-term value to the ecosystem.
